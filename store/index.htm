Digital Fluency Playing Cards

Digital Fluency Playing Cards for Year 1-10 Educators in New Zealand

The Mind Lab’s Digital Passport team is proud to introduce Digital Fluency Playing Cards, an innovative card game pack to help all Year 1-10 educators navigate the new Digital Technologies learning areas | Hangarau Matihiko tupuranga of the New Zealand curriculum.

These playing cards have been designed to support your learning as you integrate digital technologies into your classroom practice. Wherever you are in your learning journey, these cards offer exciting ways to further your development – and have some fun while doing it!

The Playing Card Suits

Question Cards offer multiple choice quiz questions to test your knowledge
Online Search Cards guide useful online research to deepen understanding
Discussion Cards promote engaging, relevant conversations around each topic
Activity Cards steer hands-on learning for real-life application

Behind the Cards

The Ministry of Education requires all teachers to be able to deliver key competencies in digital technologies by 2020. Our experts created an online course, Digital Passport, to help all schools and kura upskill in time to meet this requirement. Reaching its expected annual sign-ups in just four weeks, the team quickly realised just how important it was for teachers to have access to tools and materials that would guide their learning in this subject. The Digital Fluency Playing Cards were developed to help meet this need.
